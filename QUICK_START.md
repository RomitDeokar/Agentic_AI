## ğŸ“ Learning Resources

### Understanding the AI

**MDP (Markov Decision Process):**
- States: Where you are now
- Actions: What you can do
- Rewards: How good each action is
- Policy: Best action for each state

**Bayesian Inference:**
- Prior: What we believe before
- Likelihood: What we observe
- Posterior: Updated belief
- Confidence: How sure we are

**Reinforcement Learning:**
- Agent: The AI learner
- Environment: The world (your trip)
- Reward: Feedback signal
- Learning: Improving over time

**Multi-Agent System:**
- 7 specialized agents
- Each has unique expertise
- Collaborate to solve complex problems
- Emergent intelligent behavior
